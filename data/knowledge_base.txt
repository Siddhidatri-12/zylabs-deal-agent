Document 1: ZyLabs Overview
ZyLabs helps B2B sales teams execute deal engineering at scale.
The platform identifies accounts showing buying intent, maps decision makers, researches company initiatives, uncovers relevant business signals, and helps create highly personalized outreach.
Key capabilities:
Account research
Stakeholder discovery
Buying signal detection
Personalized outreach preparation
Deal intelligence generation
---
Document 2: ICP Definition
Ideal customers typically have:
50 to 5000 employees
Dedicated outbound sales teams
B2B sales motion
Average contract value above $10,000
Industries:
SaaS
Cybersecurity
FinTech
Cloud Infrastructure
Developer Tools
Common buyers:
VP Sales
CRO
Revenue Operations
Head of Business Development
---
Document 3: Buying Signals
Strong buying signals include:
Recent funding announcement
New executive hire
Rapid hiring growth
Geographic expansion
Product launch
New sales leadership
Weak signals include:
Generic social media activity
Website design updates
---
Document 4: Stakeholder Framework
Every account should be analyzed using the following stakeholder categories:
Champion
Economic Buyer
Technical Evaluator
Decision Maker
Influencer
---
Document 5: Outreach Personalization
Effective outreach should reference:
Company initiatives
Recent business events
Industry trends
Stakeholder responsibilities
Avoid generic personalization.
Good personalization connects business events to measurable business outcomes.
